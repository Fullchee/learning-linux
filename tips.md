### Drag and Drop Shortcuts
- **Copy the File**
    - Windows/Linux: ctrl-drag/drop
    - Mac: alt-drag/drop
- **Create an alias**
    - Windows/Linux: alt-drag/drop
    - Mac: cmd-alt-drag/drop
